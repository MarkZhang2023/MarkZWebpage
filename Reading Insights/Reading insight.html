Why classics?
I love reading novels. I enjoy the sense of emptiness that follows after finishing a classic. Classics have the power to transport my mind beyond my dimension, out of its usual boundaries. It can be frustrating when I'm halfway through a novel and don't grasp its meaning. However, the feeling of accomplishment resonates even more vehemently in my heart once I understand a motif upon completing the book.
